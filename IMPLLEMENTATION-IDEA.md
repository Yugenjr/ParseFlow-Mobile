## One important recommendation

Don't try to build **all 25 features** immediately. This document is your **product vision**, not your two-day implementation plan.

I'd define three milestones:

* **Version 1.0:** Authentication, upload, cloud storage, AI categorization, search, vault, and mobile experience.
* **Version 1.5:** DocBot, compression, scanner, notifications, and document reminders.
* **Version 2.0:** Chrome extension with secure autofill and website trust checking.

That way you're always shipping a complete product at each stage instead of chasing a massive feature list that never quite feels finished. ParseFlow has enough potential that it's worth building in layers rather than trying to cram the entire vision into one release.
